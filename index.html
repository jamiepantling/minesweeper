Sproutsweeper!

instructions:

  • There are Brussels sprouts all over the grid! Gross!
  • Find them all to stop your dinner being ruined.
  • Click a square to reveal it.
  • If it's a Brussels sprout, you lose! Ugh!
  • If it's next to a sprout, it will tell you how many sprouts surround it.
  • Right-click to flag a sprout.
  • Flag them all and you win!
  • Click the emoji to reset.
